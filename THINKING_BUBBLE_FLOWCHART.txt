ROOT CAUSE: chat.js line 300 uses .remove() instead of .style.display

WHY IT MATTERS: When thinking_done removes the indicator, there's no element left
                to restore when delegation happens. The bubble sits empty until
                delegation completes and typing dots are injected as fallback.

AFFECTED FILES:
  - chat.js (main issue in 'thinking_done' handler)
  - server.js (sends thinking_done at wrong time during delegation)

QUICK FIX: Change line 300 from indicator.remove() to indicator.style.display = 'none'

VERIFICATION: After fix, thinking indicator should:
  1. Show during initial thinking
  2. Hide during thinking_done
  3. Remain in DOM (not removed)
  4. Be restorable via display = ''
  5. Not show empty bubble during delegation
